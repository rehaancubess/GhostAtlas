Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Information We Collect

GhostAtlas collects the following information: • Location data when you submit or verify encounters • Story content and author names you provide • Device identifiers for preventing duplicate ratings • Usage data to improve the app experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: • Display your stories on the map and in the stories section • Prevent duplicate ratings and verifications • Generate AI-enhanced narratives and illustrations • Improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on AWS servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Your stories are publicly visible on the map if marked as public.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to: • Request deletion of your stories • Update your information • Opt out of data collection Contact us at rehaancubes@gmail.com for any requests.

6. Children's Privacy

GhostAtlas is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
